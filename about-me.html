About Me

This page is a window into how I approach product problems: how I reason, make trade-offs, and work with people when the path forward isn’t obvious.

Product Philosophy

Great products live at the intersection of empathy, strategy, and technical feasibility. I believe execution without strategy is noise, and strategy without execution is theory. The real work happens when both move together.

My Default Approach

  • Start with real user pain, not assumptions
  • Define success and failure before proposing solutions
  • Optimize early for learning, not perfection
  • Ship clarity, not complexity

Trade-offs I Take Seriously

I say no to features that increase cognitive load, timelines that burn teams out, and metrics that look impressive but don’t reflect real value. Sustainable progress matters more than short-term wins.

Working Experience

  • Calm in ambiguity I create structure when problems are unclear and help teams move forward with confidence.
  • Direct, but kind I value honest conversations, early feedback, and clarity without ego.
  • Opinionated, not stubborn I bring a strong point of view and change course when better evidence appears.
  • Systems thinker I zoom out to understand how decisions affect users, teams, and operations.
  • Human + technical I translate between business goals, user needs, and engineering realities.
